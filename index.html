01 · 關於比賽

01 · About

把古典學習變成可研究、可展示、可互動的 AI 作品

Turn classical learning into researchable, demonstrable and interactive AI work

本比賽是香港理工大學人文學院專為本科生舉辦的旗艦型校內活動，聚焦人工智能與古典研究的跨界融合。

This flagship Faculty of Humanities initiative centres on the intersection of artificial intelligence and classical studies.

Classics 可廣義理解為古典文本、經典思想、古代歷史文化、古籍圖像、經典翻譯與相關知識傳播。

Classics is understood broadly: classical texts, canonical thought, premodern history and culture, rare-book images, translations of classics and related knowledge communication.

09 · 常見問題

09 · FAQ

參賽前最常見的幾個判斷點

Common decision points before entering

誰可以參賽？Who is eligible to enter?

香港理工大學人文學院四個合資格本科課程的全日制本科生均可參賽。完整名單請參閱參賽資格頁面。

Full-time undergraduate students enrolled in the Faculty of Humanities at PolyU, across the four eligible programmes. Please refer to the Eligibility & Teams page for the full list.

我可以個人參賽嗎？Can I participate individually?

可以。作品可由個人或 2 至 4 人團隊提交。每名學生只可作為正式成員加入一隊。

Yes. Entries may be submitted individually or in teams of 2 to 4 members. Each student may join only one team as an official member.

我需要有編程經驗嗎？Do I need programming experience?

毋須具備高階編程能力。參賽者可從 A 組入手，善用現成 GenAI 工具。

No advanced programming experience is required. Stream A supports effective use of existing GenAI tools.

作品可以橫跨兩個組別嗎？Can a project span two streams?

可以，但須在作品摘要中說明主要評審定位。

Yes, but the project abstract should explain its principal evaluative positioning.

AI 使用聲明須包含什麼？What must the AI use statement include?

須列明生成式 AI 工具、用途、生成內容範圍，以及人手修改、驗證與判斷程度。

It must specify the tools used, purposes, scope of generated content and extent of human revision and verification.

我的知識產權會保留嗎？Will my intellectual property be retained?

會。作品著作權及知識產權原則上屬參賽者所有。

Yes. Copyright and intellectual property in submitted work shall, in principle, remain with the participants.
